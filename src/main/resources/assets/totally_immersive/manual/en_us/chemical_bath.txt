Chemical Bath
Bathing in Chemicals
<&chemical_bath>
The Chemical Bath is one of the simpler machines the engineer will find useful; it automates simple tasks, such as making Treated Wood.
To form the structure, rightclick the Redstone Engineering block at the front of the machine.
The fluid is inserted into the port on the front of the machine, while the item input is on the side with the Redstone Engineering block, and the output opposite.
